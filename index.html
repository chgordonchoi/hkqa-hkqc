About the Event

香港魁地奇總會自2023年起每年舉辦香港魁地奇盃作為香港之頂級賽事，由 Hongkong Hydras Quidditch Club 與 Victorian Dragons 角逐冠軍寶座。

The Hong Kong Quadball Association organises the Hong Kong Quadball Cup as the premier tournament annually since 2023. The Cup has been competed by the two quadball clubs in Hong Kong, the Hongkong Hydras Quidditch Club and Victorian Dragons.
